Slanguage Privacy Policy

Slanguage Privacy Policy Welcome to Slanguage (hereinafter referred to as "the App" or "this App"), a mobile application developed by the Slanguage Team (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our"). We highly value your privacy. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we collect, use, transfer, and protect your personal data. By downloading, installing, or using this App, you signify your agreement to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 1. Data Collection and Usage -Text Data: When you use the translation, contextual slang, or tone-switching features of this App, the text you enter is transmitted via a secure, encrypted connection (HTTPS) to third-party AI service providers (such as the Google Gemini API) for real-time text analysis and translation processing. We do not permanently store, retain, or utilize any of your input text on our cloud servers. -Financial and Payment Data: The subscription and payment features of this App are fully processed through the In-App Purchase (IAP) mechanisms of the Google Play Store / Apple App Store and managed via the third-party framework RevenueCat. We do not collect, store, read, or have access to your credit card numbers, bank accounts, or any real financial data. -Technical and Error Data: To optimize the user experience, we automatically collect anonymous performance data (such as crash logs, network connection errors, and HTTP status codes). This data does not contain any personally identifiable information (PII). 2. Third-Party Service Providers To maintain stable operations, optimize cross-border connectivity, and manage subscription monetization, we utilize the following trusted third-party SDKs: -Google Firebase / Crashlytics: Used for analyzing app crash causes, performance monitoring, and anonymous active user statistics. -Sentry: Used for real-time monitoring of network connectivity errors and API exceptions. -RevenueCat: Used to securely validate, manage, and synchronize your VIP subscription status. 💡 The processing of data by the aforementioned service providers is governed by their respective privacy policies. We only share de-identified data with these providers to the extent strictly necessary to achieve the functionalities stated above. 3. Data Security and Transmission We implement industry-standard Transport Layer Security (HTTPS/TLS) to encrypt all data during transmission. This ensures the security of your data in transit and protects it from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. 4. User Rights and Data Deletion Since this App operates on a registration-free (anonymous and localized) model, we do not create individual user accounts on our cloud servers. The translation text you input is released from memory immediately after the API response is generated. If you wish to cancel your subscription or manage payment records, you can do so at any time through your Apple ID or Google Account settings. You can also clear local data via the settings within this App to remove local caches. 5. Policy Changes and Contact Information We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. The amended policy will become effective immediately upon publication. If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please feel free to contact us via the official support email listed on our App Store / Google Play page.
